Translation: 高铁

Writing：
The Smartphone Addiction

Translation

中国目前拥有世界上最大最快的高速铁路网。
China has owned the fastest and the 规模庞大的 high speed train net.
China now has the largest and fastest high-speed rail net work in the world.

高铁列车的运行速度还将继续提升，更多的城市将修建高铁站。高铁大大缩短了人们出行的时间。
The running speed of high-speed trian will still be improed, more and more high-speed train stations are building up. The high-speed short the traveling time extremely.
The speed of CRH(China Railway High-speed trian) will continue to increase and more cities will build high-speed rail stations. The CHR train has greatly reduced people’s travel time.

相对飞机而言，高铁列车的突出优势在于准时，因为基本不受天气或交通管制的影响。
Compareing to airplane, the most advantage of CRH is time-keeping，because it seldom ifluenced by the weather or trasptation.
Compared with the airplane, the outstanding advantage of the CHR train is punctuality, because it is basically not affected by weather or traffic control.

高铁极大地改变了中国人的生活方式。如今，它已经成为了很多人商务旅行的首选交通工具。
CRH train has changed Chinese lifestyle considerably. Nowdays, it becomes the first transptation vehicle when people have a business.
It has transformed the way Chinese people live and become the favorite option for business travelers today.

越来越多的人也在假日乘高铁外出旅游。
More and more people travel outside by CRH on vacation
More and more people also travel by CRH train during holidays.

还有不少年轻人选择在一个城市工作而在邻近城市居住，每天乘高铁上下班。
There are some youth work in a city but live in another, and taking CRH train to and from work.
Many young people choose to work in one city but la nearive in by one since they can commute by CHR train every day.

Highlights

1.the favorite option for
2.commute:[kəˈmjuːt] v.上下班；n.上下班的路程

Writing

  • Explain the phenomenon
  • The reasons for this social problem
  • Measures to be taken to solve it

It is an undeniable fact that smartphones have become an integral part of people’s life: They use it to scanning payment, sending messages…… But plenty of people have become addicted to smartphones which has already ailed their daily life.

Model Essay:
The popularity of smartphones makes the smartphone addiction increasingly
commonplace[1]. While we enjoy the convenience brought by smartphones, the overuse of smartphones exerts[2] multiple adverse[3] impacts on our life.

First of all, an excessive[4] use of smartphones diminishes[5] our ability to concentrate. Before getting up or going to sleep, we spend a long time surfing the Internet. When we are at work, in meetings and even when we are making breakfast, we check our phones for calls or messages. What’ more, the smartphone addiction poses a danger to our interpersonal communication. We’ve all seen some couples sittig together in a restuarant, ignoring each other and engaging in their smartphones.

To get rid of the smartphone addiction, I suggest we should be disciplined[6] and limit our use of smartphones step by step. The following tips may be helpful: Set rules for when we can use our smartphones; turn off our phones at certain times of the day; don’t bring our phones or tablets[7] to bedroom.

Details

[1] : a.平凡的，常见的
[2] : v.运用，施加[ɪɡˈzɜːt]
[3] : a.不利的，反面的
[4] : a.过分的，过度的
[5] : v.减少，降低[dɪˈmɪnɪʃ]
[6] : v.训练，处罚，自我管控；n.训练，行为准则，纪律[ˈdɪsəplɪn]
[7] : n.药丸；牌匾[ˈtæblət]
